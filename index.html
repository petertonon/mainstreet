Our Work

Mainstreet Capital Partners is a real estate investment and management company that acquires and develops office, industrial and multi-family properties in the Southeastern and Southwestern United States. Established in 1999 by Paul J. Kilgallon, we joint venture our investments with Institutional partners in high growth Metropolitan areas covered by our Principals with over 90 years of combined investment experience. Serving as the General Partner, our investments are managed by our subsidiary, Mainstreet Real Estate Services. Our extensive experience and market knowledge, along with hands-on management serve to create successful repositioning and value enhancements in 38 transactions totaling $1B of investments.
